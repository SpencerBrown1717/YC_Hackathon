How it works

Three steps, sixty seconds.

01
Dial the number
Tap call. AgentPhone routes you to an AI agent configured to channel the energy of a famously direct YC investor.
02
Pitch your idea
Sixty seconds. What you're building, who it's for, and why now. No slides. No hedging. Just talk.
03
Get the verdict
Tough questions. Honest reactions. A thumbs up, a thumbs down, or a "talk to users first."
What you'll hear

The kind of tough love only a phone call can deliver.

i.
Probing questions about your wedge, your users, and your unit economics.
ii.
Pattern matching against thousands of startups it's been trained to recognize.
iii.
Blunt feedback when your idea sounds like a feature, not a company.
iv.
Encouragement when you've actually found something rare.
v.
A final verdict. Good idea, bad idea, or "do things that don't scale."
